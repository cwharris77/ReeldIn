What is ReeldIn?

Movies are a highly valued creative outlet and pastime, but frequently, it's hard to choose one. With millions available and more releasing daily, the hurdle of picking something to watch can be daunting. ReeldIn intends to match users with movies they'll enjoy. Our product is sleek, quick. and efficient. We use a combination of user input and machine learning to provide the best recommendations possible. We're here to make your movie-watching experience as enjoyable as possible.
